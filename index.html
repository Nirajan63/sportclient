Our Story

Rooted in Surkhet,
Built for Adventure

Sports With Us was founded by a team of passionate adventurers in Birendranagar — one of Nepal's most underrated adventure hubs. We believe the Karnali region holds some of the country's most exhilarating experiences, and we share them responsibly.

Every trip is designed with deep local knowledge, certified safety standards, and genuine care for both our clients and the environment we operate in.
